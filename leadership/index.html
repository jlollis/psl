  1. Board Chairman

    Rick Nucci

    CEO and co-founder of Guru

    Rick is CEO and co-founder of Guru. Previously, he co-founded Boomi in 2000 (acquired by Dell in 2010), and served as General Manager following the acquisition.

  2. Board Member

    Josh Kopelman

    Partner & Founder at FirstRound Capital

    Josh is partner and founder at FirstRound Capital. Previously, Josh co-founded Infonautics Corporation and took it public in 1996. He founded Half.com (acquired by eBay in 2000) and helped found TurnTide (acquired by Symantec in 2004).

  3. Board Member

    Gabriel Weinberg

    Founder & CEO of DuckDuckGo

    Gabe is founder & CEO of DuckDuckGo and co-author of Traction: A Startup Guide to Getting Customers. Previously he was the Co-founder & CEO of Opobox (acquired by United Online in 2006).

  4. Board Member

    Wil Reynolds

    Founder of SEER Interactive

    Wil Reynolds founded SEER Interactive, an online marketing agency, in 2002. He also sits on the advisory board of Convenant House, an organization that works with runaway youth in the Philly area.

  5. Board Member

    Amanda Christini, M.D.

    Co-Founder of BlackFynn

    Amanda has 20 years' experience in the biotechnology industry, clinical medicine, the business and strategy of medicine and leveraging technology to change clinical practice. Prior to joining Blackfynn, Amanda was Director of Strategic Initiatives at the Penn Medicine Center for Healthcare Innovation.

  6. Board Member

    Emily Foote

    Co-Founder of Practice

    Driven by the ideal of providing equal education opportunities for all learners, Emily co-founded Practice. A former Teach for America and KIPP teacher, lawyer and luddite, Emily has embraced technology’s potential to help educators scale great teaching and learning. Emily received her B.A. and M.S. Ed. from the University of Pennsylvania and her J.D. from Drexel Law.

  7. Board Secretary

    Danielle Cohn

    Entrepreneurial Engagement, Comcast

    Danielle is the first entrepreneurs' advocate at Comcast NBCUniversal, focused on startup partnerships and developing Comcast's accelerator in the Comcast Innovation + Technology Center. Danielle helped launch the City's Startup PHL seed fund. Danielle is cofounder of HOSPITALity – a tech startup aiming to improve patient experiences in hospitals through hospitality and communications.

  8. President

Previously Brock was SVP/Chief Digital Officer of Petsmart after selling Pet360 where he was CEO the prior 5 years.
